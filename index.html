The Story

Seven years
BEHIND THE CHAIR.

Tropical Barbershop is built in honour of the people who raised me, my grandparents, born in the twenties and thirties. They raised me on true craftsmanship, attention to detail, and the idea that things should be built to last.

Longevity over trends.

A botanical room, a careful hand, an honest cut. A place where real people can sit down and have an escape from the real world.

Craftsmanship Slow work, sharp tools.
Attention Every detail, every chair.
Longevity Built to last. Not to trend.
Honest Care Simple, chosen with care.
